The country needs skilled personnel, be it in the area of Advertising, Public Relations, Reporting, Editing, Photography, Media Management, Audio-Visual Programming, Radio News casting, Anchoring, Video Jockeying, Radio Jockeying, Film Making and so on. Our various courses have been designed with an eye on future employment demand. For the realization of the dreams of the students the Institute approaches the reputed organizations of print and electronic media for accepting the services of our trained media personnel. NIMS maintains close contacts and professional relationships with a large number of news organizations, academic institutions and corporations. Preference will be given to qualified and deserving students of the Institute to be engaged in the employment of NAXATRA NEWS TV channel, and other upcoming TV channels of the company and Odia daily newspaper: NAKSHATRA JYOTI. NIMS placement process runs throughout the academic year as alongside academics the students are involved in the various units of the NK Media ventures comprising of its news channel and newspaper.
